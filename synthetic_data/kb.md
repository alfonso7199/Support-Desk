# Support Knowledge Base (synthetic)

## KB-01 Returns & refunds
Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Refunds are
issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after the item is
received. Opened consumable products are non-refundable.

## KB-02 Shipping times & delays
Standard shipping is 3-5 business days; express is 1-2. If an order is more than 3
business days past its estimated delivery date, support may offer a reshipment or a
free-shipping voucher and open a carrier investigation.

## KB-03 Double charge / billing
If a customer is charged twice for one order, confirm the duplicate transaction IDs,
refund the duplicate immediately, and apologize. Duplicate-charge refunds do not
require manager approval up to 200 EUR.

## KB-04 Password reset & account access
Customers can reset their password from the login page via "Forgot password". If the
reset email does not arrive, verify the account email and resend; never share or set
passwords on the customer's behalf.

## KB-05 Warranty & replacements
Hardware has a 24-month warranty. For a defective item within warranty, offer a free
replacement once the fault is described; advanced replacement (ship before return)
is allowed for customers in good standing.

## KB-06 Feature requests
Feature requests are logged to the product board and acknowledged. Do not promise
delivery dates. Thank the customer and share the public roadmap link.
